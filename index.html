About

I am a passionate Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer that's excited to share all my projects with the world! I loved the idea that I could better understand our reality through data and it's an absolute pleasure to be able to work on projects I find compelling enough to want to explore through data analysis, models, algorithms and fun data visualizations!

I am eager to find employment with a company that's looking to expand its data and machine learning applications. Having worked in the localization industry, I am particularly interested in NLP and NLG, but I am open to any data driven environment that provides an intellectual safe space to learn and experiment through data!

After a 10 year career in account management, customer success and sales, I was able to make a career transition into a dream field and I am looking forward to my next challenge.

Data science, machine learning, NLP, with strong soft skills background of 10+ years in account management and 6+ years in sales.
